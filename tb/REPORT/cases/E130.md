## Intent

- **Scenario:** sector-boundary wrap with pop ownership: pop owns sector 7 (addresses 448..511) and `write_pointer` wraps from 511 (sector 7) to 0 (sector 0)
- **Primary checks:** the address-511 push waits behind `push_write_sector_locked=1`; the cycle `write_pointer` wraps to 0 the lock falls and `push_write_grant=1` fires; catches a wrap that would still see the locked sector via stale `addr_sector(write_pointer-1)`
