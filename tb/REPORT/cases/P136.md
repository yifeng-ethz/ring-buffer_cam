## Intent

- **Scenario:** non-power-of-two depth sector geometry under random load: `pool=4` random multi-key, 200k hits, depth=384 (so `ADDR_W=9` and the sector partition is uneven across the address space)
- **Primary checks:** `addr_sector(write_pointer)` never produces an out-of-range index; lock-mask popcount tracks distinct snapshot sectors exactly; final push/pop accounting closes; catches a width assumption that only worked at depth 512
